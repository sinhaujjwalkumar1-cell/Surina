4. Provider verification and the personal-performance rule

Providers can complete Aadhaar-based KYC verification to display an "Aadhaar OK" badge and to show interest on requests posted to the feed. Regardless of verification status, every provider profile is bound by one non-negotiable rule:

Provider Declaration: "I confirm that I will personally visit and provide this service myself. I will not send an employee or any other person on my behalf."
  • The individual who creates and verifies a provider profile must be the same individual who personally performs the service.
  • A provider may not use their profile to send an employee, family member, or any other person to perform work on their behalf.
  • Ownership of tools or equipment is not required to register — a provider may own, rent, or borrow the tools needed for their trade. Arranging tools and materials is solely the provider's own responsibility.
  • For skill-based labour categories (for example, mason or general labour work), where the "tool" is the provider's own hands-on skill and materials are typically available at the worksite, no tool-ownership question applies — only the personal-performance declaration matters.
  • Service By Local is not a platform for individuals seeking employment or job placement. Anyone looking to be hired as staff by a third party may not use the Platform in that capacity.
  • Violation of this rule may result in immediate and permanent suspension of a provider's account.
