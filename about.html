About Us

A zero-commission hyperlocal services marketplace.

Service By Local is a platform where verified local service providers and nearby customers connect directly — without a middleman, an agent, or a commission taken from either side.

What Service By Local is

Service By Local is operated by SBL Digital Corporation Private Limited. It gives every independent local provider their own digital profile — a real online identity, discoverable directly by nearby customers — for providers who deliver their service or supply on-site, in person — such as appliance repair technicians, electricians, plumbers, masons, home-visit salon artists, local material suppliers (like sand and gravel), and owner-drivers renting out their own vehicle.

The platform does not take a commission on any job, and does not dispatch or assign work. Every provider profile must be operated personally by the same individual who performs the service or supply — a provider cannot send an employee or another person on their behalf. Service By Local is not a staffing or recruitment platform: a shop or business cannot use it to hire workers for their premises. It exists for independent individuals to be found directly by nearby customers.

Our Mission

To give every independent skilled worker in India — the electrician, the mason, the driver, the local supplier — the same digital visibility and recognition that businesses in big cities have long had, with zero commission taken from their earnings.

Our Vision for Bharat

A Bharat where a mistri in a small town is as easy to find online as a shop in a big city — where skill, not connections, decides who gets found and who gets work.

What we stand for

Three ideas that don't change

Zero commission, always

Providers keep everything they earn. The platform makes no cut from either side of a job.

Real people, not staff

Every profile is one verified person who personally performs the service — never an assigned employee.

Built for small towns first

Designed around tier-2/3 India's reality, not retrofitted from a metro-city product.
