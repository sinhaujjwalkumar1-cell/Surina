Why this matters

A doctor has a Google listing. A hotel has one too. The electrician down the street never did.

Frequently asked

Common questions about Service By Local

What is Service By Local?

Service By Local is a zero-commission hyperlocal services marketplace. It connects independent local providers — including on-site technicians, home-visit artists, local material suppliers, and vehicle owner-drivers — directly with nearby customers, without any middleman or commission. It is not a staffing or recruitment platform.

Does Service By Local charge commission on jobs?

No. Service By Local does not take any commission from providers or customers. Providers keep 100% of what they earn.

Is Service By Local a job marketplace or staffing agency?

No. Service By Local is not a job marketplace and does not dispatch staff. Every provider profile must be personally operated by the same individual who performs the service — a provider cannot send an employee or another person on their behalf.

How are providers verified?

Providers complete Aadhaar-based identity verification to show interest on requests and unlock an "Aadhaar OK" badge. Only their name, age, and gender are verified and shown — the Aadhaar number and photo are never displayed. Providers can also upload a skill certificate, reviewed and approved by the SBL team, to earn a "Skill Certified" badge.

How does a customer request a service?

A customer can open a provider's full profile and send a request directly to that one provider, or post a request to the local feed so nearby verified providers can see it and show interest. Either way, the provider is notified instantly, and the customer's exact contact details are shared only once they confirm a provider.

Can a customer call or message a provider directly?

Yes. From a provider's profile, a customer can call, message on WhatsApp, or use in-app chat directly — in addition to sending a job request.

What service categories does Service By Local cover?

Eight categories: Appliance Repair, Home Services, Construction, Rental Vehicle, Daily Needs, Beauty & Grooming, Event Services, and Education & Tuition — split further into 66 sub-categories.

Is Service By Local available as a mobile app?

Yes — Service By Local is available on Google Play, in addition to this website.
